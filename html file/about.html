OUR

STORY

Vortex Esports is a premier esports organization for passionate Filipino gamers, based in the Philippines. It was founded in 2024 by Web Development, who saw potential in FIlipino talents and thus created an organization wherein this potential an be fully actualized by giving talents the proper support they need.

VISION

Vortex Esports strives to be a worldwide-leading esports organization dedicated to winning championships, bringing ideas to life, and empowering Filipino Gamers

MISSION

Vortex Esports is committed to serving Filipinos with a passion for gaming. Through dedication in developing talents and providing them with the outmost support, Vortex strives to champion in everything we set our hands to, whether this be esports, content creation, or creative ventures.
